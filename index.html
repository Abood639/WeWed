Meet Coach Lovey

We Connect. We Prevent. We Build.

Yolonda “Coach Lovey” Young is the founder of WeWed, LLC and a Certified Relationship Coach with a Bachelor’s degree in Clinical Psychology. Her work focuses on helping singles, couples, and groups build healthier communication, stronger connection, and practical relationship habits before things break down.

Through WeWed, Coach Lovey provides relationship coaching that is preventative, practical, and forward-focused. Her sessions are designed to help clients recognize patterns, strengthen communication, build self-awareness, and create healthier ways of showing up in their relationships.

Our Philosophy

What Makes This Approach Different

Coach Lovey blends relational insight, communication strategy, guided conversation, and practical tools to support personal and relational growth.

This work focuses on everyday moments: how you speak, listen, respond, pause, repair, set boundaries, and make decisions. Small shifts in understanding and communication can create meaningful changes in how relationships feel over time.

The goal is not to stay stuck in the past. The goal is to understand what matters, recognize what needs to change, and build healthier habits moving forward.

Core Distinction

Coaching, Not Therapy

WeWed is not therapy, counseling, clinical psychotherapy, or mental health treatment. Coaching services are educational, facilitative, and growth-focused.

Coach Lovey does not diagnose, treat mental health conditions, provide crisis intervention, or replace the support of a licensed mental health professional. Instead, WeWed focuses on prevention, maintenance, communication, clarity, boundaries, and intentional relationship growth.

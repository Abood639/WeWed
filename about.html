Meet Coach Lovey

Applying preventative care systems to rebuild relational health, intimacy, and trust.

Why I Do This Work

Coach Lovey’s passion for relationships did not begin in a textbook. It came from lived experience, personal observation, and a deep desire to understand why people can love each other but still struggle to communicate, connect, and maintain emotional safety.

Growing up around conflict gave her an early awareness of how much communication shapes relationships, homes, and personal identity. During her own season of singleness, she became intentional about studying what creates healthy love, strong partnership, and long-term relational stability.

That journey shaped the heart of WeWed: helping people connect with intention, prevent unnecessary breakdowns, and build healthier patterns for the future.

Relational Prevention

Coach Lovey has spent 20 years focusing on prevention in health and relationship wellness, building WeWed as a preventative support system for individuals and couples.

Methodology

How I Work

Coach Lovey’s coaching style is interactive, conversational, and deeply practical. Sessions are not just about talking through problems; they are designed to help clients see patterns, connect the dots, and apply what they learn in everyday life.

She often uses relatable stories, parables, examples, and real-life scenarios to make communication and relationship concepts easier to understand. This approach helps clients feel seen, stay engaged, and walk away with language, tools, and perspective they can actually use.

Rather than simply giving advice, Coach Lovey guides clients through reflection, honest conversation, practical exercises, and intentional discussion. Her goal is to help clients understand what is happening beneath the surface, communicate more clearly, and move forward with confidence.

Her gift is noticing what is often left unsaid and helping clients put language to the thoughts, emotions, and patterns that may be affecting how they show up in relationships.

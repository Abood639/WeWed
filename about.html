Meet Coach Lovey

Applying preventative care systems to rebuild relational health, intimacy, and trust.

My Journey to Relational Wellness

"My passion for relationships didn't come from a textbook—it came from my own upbringing."

Growing up around constant fighting, I knew early on that I wanted something different for my life. During my season of singleness, I went on a mission to discover what it truly takes to build a thriving, healthy marriage.

Along the way, I saw so many couples staying in unhappy, draining dynamics simply because they were afraid of change, even when they knew the situation was bad for them.

Combining my 20 years of professional background in preventative care with a lifetime of studying what makes love actually work, I help couples stop settling for the status quo. My gift is seeing and feeling what is left unsaid, peeling back the layers to speak up for the parts of your relationship that have been buried.

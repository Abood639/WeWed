Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about relational wellness coaching.

What is relational prevention?

+
Unlike traditional couples therapy which often acts as emergency damage control, prevention focuses on establishing healthy communication habits, stress boundaries, and constructive tools before gridlocked issues arise. It builds protective behaviors.

How many sessions are recommended?

+
Coach Lovey recommends starting with a 6-session package. This provides enough time to identify recurring conflict loops, establish healthy routines, and build habits that ensure long-term stability and connection.

Can individuals book coaching sessions?

+
Yes, absolutely. We offer individual tracks for both regular preventative coaching and communication deep-dives. These sessions are perfect for identifying personal triggers, finding your voice, and sorting through feelings before bringing them back to your partner.

How do intentional communication groups work?

+
Cohorts meet weekly online in a supportive, low-pressure environment. Led by Coach Lovey, these sessions cover core topics like conflict strategies, boundary setting, and daily habits. You can share, ask questions, or simply listen and take the advice that works for your life.
